Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Who We Are

FindHoodCleaning.com is operated by Simple Rabbit LLC, a marketing agency based in Bergen County, New Jersey. This website is a free directory that helps restaurant owners and commercial kitchen operators find hood cleaning services in their area. You can reach us at [email protected].

What Information We Collect

Business listing submissions: If you submit your business through our "List Your Business" form, we collect the information you provide, including your business name, contact name, phone number, email address, business address, website, services offered, and any additional notes. This information is submitted through Formspree and sent directly to our email. We use it solely to create and maintain your directory listing.

Analytics data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous data such as which pages you visit, how long you stay, what device and browser you use, and your general geographic location. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not: require you to create an account, collect payment information, use cookies for advertising, sell or share your personal information with third parties, or track you across other websites.

How We Use Your Information

Business listing information is used only to create and display your listing on our directory. We may contact you at the email or phone number you provided if we have questions about your listing. Analytics data is used to understand site traffic and improve the directory.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to measure website traffic. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Formspree: Our listing submission form is processed through Formspree. Their privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may collect your IP address. Google's privacy policy applies.

Business Listing Information

The business listings displayed on this site are sourced from publicly available information via the Google Places API, including business names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, ratings, and reviews. If you are a business owner and want your listing updated or removed, email us at [email protected] and we will process your request within 7 business days.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). In either case, contact us at the email above to exercise your rights.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information submitted through our site. Our website is served over HTTPS, and form submissions are transmitted securely through Formspree. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
