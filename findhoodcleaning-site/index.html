Why It Matters

Hood Cleaning Is Not Optional

Fire Prevention

Grease buildup in exhaust systems is the leading cause of restaurant fires. Regular professional cleaning removes this hazard before it becomes a crisis.

Insurance Compliance

Most commercial insurance policies require documented, professional hood cleaning on a regular schedule. Without it, your coverage could be void when you need it most.

Health Inspections

A dirty exhaust system is a red flag for health inspectors. Clean hoods and ducts help you pass inspections and avoid costly violations or shutdowns.
